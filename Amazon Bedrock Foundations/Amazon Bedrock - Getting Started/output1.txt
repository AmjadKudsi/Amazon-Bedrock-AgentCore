# AWS Bedrock: A Beginner's Guide

## What is AWS Bedrock?

AWS Bedrock is Amazon's service that gives you easy access to powerful AI models through a simple interface. Think of it as a "library" of pre-built AI brains that you can use in your applications without needing to build or train AI models yourself.
